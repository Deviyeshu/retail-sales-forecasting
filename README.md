# 🛒 Retail Sales Forecasting & Performance Analysis

> Analysis of 9,800 retail transactions with ARIMA-based 
> 6-month sales forecasting using Python and Tableau.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective
- Which product categories and regions drive the most sales?
- What are the seasonal sales patterns across 4 years?
- What will sales look like for the next 6 months?

---

## 📈 Key Findings
- **$2.26M** total sales across 4 years (2015-2018)
- **Technology** is the top category at $827,456
- **Phones** and **Chairs** are top sub-categories
- Sales show consistent **November peak** every year
- **Standard Class** shipping dominates at $1.34M
- **ARIMA forecast** predicts 2019 sales stabilising 
  around $40K-$105K per month

---

## 🔧 Tools Used
- Python, Pandas, Matplotlib
- Statsmodels (ARIMA forecasting)
- Tableau Public (interactive dashboard)
- Dataset: Superstore Sales from Kaggle

## 💡 Recommendations
1. **Stock up Technology products** before November — 
   consistent peak sales every year in Q4
2. **Focus on West and East regions** — they contribute 
   55% of total sales combined
3. **Promote Standard Class shipping** deals — customers 
   prefer it overwhelmingly at 59% of all orders
